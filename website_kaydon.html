Westminster is located in Inner London on the north bank of the River Thames, immediately west of the City of London. The borough covers approximately 21.5 km² and has city status, granted officially in 1540 and transferred to the modern borough upon its creation in 1965 following the merger of Westminster, Paddington, and St Marylebone. Its local authority is Westminster City Council, headquartered at City Hall on Victoria Street, with council meetings held at Westminster Council House or Marylebone Town Hall.
